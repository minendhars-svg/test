Programs of Study

Our department provides an undergraduate major in Computer Science along with two graduate degree options: Computer Science, and Computer Information Systems and Cybersecurity.

Faculty and Staff

The faculty and staff in the Computer Science Department are committed to helping every student succeed. You can reach us by phone, email, or in person for academic support and guidance.

Scholarships

A variety of scholarships and assistantship opportunities are available for both undergraduate and graduate students enrolled in our Computer Science and Cybersecurity programs.

Academic Advising

Our academic advisors are dedicated to helping students plan their coursework, stay on track, and achieve your career goals. Combining Computer Science with a minor like Mathematics enhances your career opportunities.

Student Clubs and Organizations

Students can participate in several active organizations, including the Mathematics Club, Computer Science Club, and Engineering Club.

Research Internships and Seminars

Faculty members conduct research across multiple computing and mathematical fields. Students are encouraged to join projects and attend seminars to gain real-world experience.
